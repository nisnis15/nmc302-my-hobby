SOUSOU NO FRIEREN

Sousou no Frieren is a calm yet emotional anime about an elf mage named Frieren who once helped defeat a great evil with her hero friends. But since elves live much longer than humans, she outlives them and begins a journey to understand human emotions and memories. The story is slow and peaceful, but also deep and touching. It shows how Frieren grows by meeting new people and remembering her old friends. The animation is beautiful, and the music is soft and emotional.
